Privacy Policy

Effective Date: December 2025
Last Updated: December 2025

Your privacy is our priority. SummarizeIt is designed with privacy at its core. All article summarization and sentiment analysis happens entirely on your device using Apple Intelligence. We do not collect, store, or transmit any of your data.

Privacy at a Glance

  • All processing happens on your device (summarization, sentiment analysis, text-to-speech)
  • No data is sent to external servers
  • No personal information is collected
  • No tracking or analytics
  • No third-party services

Information We Do NOT Collect

SummarizeIt does not collect, store, or transmit any of the following:

  • Personal information (name, email, phone number, etc.)
  • Article content or text you summarize
  • Browsing history or website URLs
  • Device identifiers or IP addresses
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Summaries or sentiment analysis results
  • Any other user data

How SummarizeIt Works

When you use SummarizeIt to summarize an article:

  1. Text Extraction: The extension extracts text from the current webpage using JavaScript running locally in your browser
  2. On-Device Processing: The text is sent to Apple Intelligence (Foundation Models) running entirely on your device
  3. Local Analysis: Apple Intelligence generates a summary and sentiment analysis without any data leaving your device
  4. Display Results: The summary is displayed in your browser
  5. Text-to-Speech (Optional): If you use the Listen feature, your device's built-in text-to-speech engine reads the summary aloud. This also happens entirely on your device
  6. No Storage: Nothing is saved or transmitted. When you close the summary, it's gone

Apple Intelligence & Privacy

SummarizeIt uses Apple's on-device Foundation Models (Apple Intelligence) for all processing. According to Apple's privacy principles:

  • All processing happens on your device
  • No data is sent to Apple's servers
  • Apple does not collect or store your content
  • Models run entirely offline once downloaded

For more information about Apple Intelligence privacy, visit: apple.com/privacy

Permissions Required

SummarizeIt requires the following Safari permissions to function:

Access to Webpage Content

Why: To extract article text for summarization
Scope: Only when you explicitly activate the extension
Data handling: Text is processed locally and never stored or transmitted

Native Messaging

Why: To communicate between the Safari extension and the native app component that accesses Apple Intelligence
Scope: Communication happens only on your device
Data handling: Messages contain only the text to summarize and results, never leave your device

Text-to-Speech

Why: To read summaries aloud using your device's built-in speech synthesis
Scope: Uses system-level text-to-speech, entirely on-device
Data handling: Summary text is processed by your device's speech engine, never transmitted externally

  • Maintain any databases

When you close a summary, it is immediately discarded from memory.
